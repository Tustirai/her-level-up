Today's Devotion:

God has gifted each of us with unique talents and skills that make us come alive when we do them. These gifts are not just for our own enjoyment but are meant to be used to further God's purposes and advance His kingdom.

1 Peter 4:10 (NIV) says, 'Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others, as faithful stewards of Godâ€™s grace in its various forms.'

We should use our gifts to serve others as faithful stewards of God's grace. We should not be modest about our gifts but should be confident in them for God's glory. We need to think about our gifts in relation to the kingdom of God and how we can use them to benefit the church, the community, and the world. We should seek to be active participants in the kingdom of God using the tools that God specifically chose for us. It's important to remember that our gifts are not just for our own benefit but are meant to be shared with others. We should use our gifts to bring light, truth, and positive influence to the world. Let us focus on the little things and find joy in the simplest of things. We should do kind things for others, practice mindfulness and meditation, spend more time with family and friends, and identify and set goals. When we use our gifts to serve others, we are fulfilling God's purpose for our lives and advancing His kingdom.
